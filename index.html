La Réunion, France

La Réunion is a French island and a department in the Indian Ocean. It is renowned for its rainforest-covered volcanic interior, coral reefs, and beaches. Its most iconic landmark is Piton de la Fournaise, an active volcano rising to an altitude of 2,632 meters that can be climbed. Piton des Neiges—a massive extinct volcano—and Réunion's three calderas (natural amphitheaters formed by collapsed volcanoes) are also popular spots for climbing.
